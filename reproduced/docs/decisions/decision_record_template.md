# Decision Record Template

Use this template to document consequential choices so future contributors can trace context, options, and follow-up actions. Copy the structure below into a new Markdown file under `../decisions/` whenever you formalize a decision.

---

## Metadata
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Owner:** Name or team responsible for the decision
- **Related PR/Issue:** URL or identifier for supporting discussion
- **Evidence:** Commands, reports, or source documents that support the decision

## Problem Statement
Describe the trigger for this decision, the constraints that shaped the discussion, and the success criteria for a good outcome.

## Options Considered
1. **Option A** – Summary of approach, anticipated benefits, and trade-offs.
2. **Option B** – Summary of approach, anticipated benefits, and trade-offs.
3. *(Add more options as needed.)*

## Decision
State the selected option and the rationale behind the choice, including any dissenting opinions that should be recorded.

## Consequences & Follow-up
- Immediate actions or migrations required
- Longer-term monitoring or review checkpoints
- Related documents, scripts, or status notes to update

## Status
Track whether follow-up actions are complete, in progress, or blocked. Include owners and due dates for open items.

---

> **Reminder:** Update `../README.md` or relevant status reports if the decision changes contributor onboarding, QA, or release processes.
